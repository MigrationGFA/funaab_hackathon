🌿 About The Hackathon

ABOUT THE HACKATHON

The Agro-Tech Hackathon is an innovative event aimed at bringing together students, researchers, Alumni and industry professionals to address the problems in the farming ecosystem through technological and sustainable solutions.

By leveraging cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI), the Internet of Things (IoT), and blockchain, participants will have the opportunity to create impactful solutions that solves problem, enhance productivity, sustainability, and efficiency in agriculture.

🌿 Eligibility

PARTICIPANT SELECTION CRITERIA

The following key areas will be considered during participant selection for the Hackathon:

01

Knowledge

Depth of understanding of agricultural business, problem statement, and emerging technology ideas.

02

Team

A team of 3–5 persons with combined skills and expertise in both agriculture and technology ecosystems.

03

Product Idea

Critical description and feasibility of the proposed problem and solution.

04

Innovative Diversity

Inclusion of diverse skills, gender (especially female founders), and innovative perspectives.

05

Potential Contribution

Measurable impact and contribution to the agricultural innovation ecosystem.

06

Experience & Proximity

Previous innovation experience and proximity to implementing suggested solutions.
