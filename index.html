ABOUT THE HACKATHON

The Agro-Tech Hackathon is an innovative event aimed at bringing together students, researchers, Alumni and industry professionals to address the problems in the farming ecosystem through technological and sustainable solutions.

By leveraging cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI), the Internet of Things (IoT), and blockchain, participants will have the opportunity to create impactful solutions that solves problem, enhance productivity, sustainability, and efficiency in agriculture.

OBJECTIVES

Industry Integration

Provide a platform for students and researchers to showcase their skills, encouraging collaboration between academia and industry.

Skills Development

Promote awareness of sustainable and smart farming practices. Develop practical solutions to real-world agricultural challenges.

Innovation Incubation

Encourage creative solutions and ideas that drive agricultural transformation and sustainability.

Agro-Industry Integration

Promote collaboration and awareness of sustainable and smart farming practices through industry engagement.
